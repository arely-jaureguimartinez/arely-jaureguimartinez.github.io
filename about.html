~About Arely~

If the title is not clear enough for you, well
my name is Arely. I am currently a first year SMASH scholar
with quite a lot of enthusiasm. I try to maintain my positive
attitude as much as I can. I consider myself mature at mind,
but childish at energy, only sometimes.

I really enjoy cartoon characters, so please enjoy this gif of my
beloved winnie the pooh, a cuddly cute bear.

I enjoy reading poetry and a genre
of poetry I enjoy is those old classic poems
such as poems by Shakespeare and Pablo Neruda.
I feel like I enjoy these poems because they are
corny, so here goes a poem by Skakespeare named
Sonnet 130:

My mistress' eyes are nothing like the sun; Coral is far more red than her lips' red; If snow be white, why then her breasts are dun; If hairs be wires, black wires grow on her head. I have seen roses damask'd, red and white, But no such roses see I in her cheeks; And in some perfumes is there more delight Than in the breath that from my mistress reeks. I love to hear her speak, yet well I know That music hath a far more pleasing sound; I grant I never saw a goddess go; My mistress, when she walks, treads on the ground: And yet, by heaven, I think my love as rare As any she belied with false compare.
